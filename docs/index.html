Buying guide

Lighting questions shoppers ask before checkout.

How high should a pendant hang?

Leave 30 to 36 inches between a counter and shade. For dining tables, keep the lowest edge around 30 inches above the tabletop.

Warm white or tuneable white?

Use 2700K for evening ambience, 3000K for most living rooms, and tuneable white where one room shifts from task work to dinner.

How wide should a chandelier be?

Add the room length and width in feet, then use that number in inches as a quick diameter guide for the fixture.
